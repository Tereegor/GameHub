Frequently Asked Questions

What is GameHub?

GameHub is a platform for gamers to buy, sell, and trade in-game items and services.

How do I create an account?

Click 'Register' on the homepage, fill out the form, and submit it to create your account.

How can I contact support?

Use the chat feature to message an admin or super admin for assistance.

How do I deposit money?

Go to your profile, select the 'Deposit' tab, and enter the amount you wish to add.
